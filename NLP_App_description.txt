NLP App: 
Product: A NLP tool for developpers, and laymen. Analyze text, audio, or video data by picking and choosing your analysis type
from wordclouds, to topic extraction. 

App processes: 

1) Choose for developers or users

1) Takes media as input in 2 formats (for now):
	- Text
	- Youtube video

2) Choose your analysis type: 
	- Sentiment analysis (positive, negative, nuetral)
	- Word grams (uni, bi, tri, ngrams)
	- Wordcloud
	- Topic modeling
	

3) Choose your output type: 
	- Python Notebook (for developers)
	- Visuals (for users)
